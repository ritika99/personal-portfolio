About me

Hi! My name is Ritika Dhiman. I am a Full Stack Developer. During my internships with India's Premium Research Organization, DRDO, and American Express, I analyzed the features of real-time systems and built predictive models to develop highly accurate and time-efficient applications.

It was during my undergraduate studies in Computer Science and Engineering at Panjab University where a broad yet intricate structure provided me a stable foundation in logic and problem-solving. Further, my genuine curiosity aided in transforming this knowledge into a consistent grade — a 9.27/10 CGPA and ranking in the top 5% of the class. Further, becoming the first woman chairperson of ‘ACM Student-Chapter’ at my institution and winning the prestigious ‘GHCI'19 and GHCI'20 Scholarship’ not only ameliorated my technical knowledge but also enhanced my networking and leadership capabilities as a woman in STEM. Additionally, I gained hands-on learning experience through ‘ACM India Winter School’ on Hybrid Cloud and Natural Language Processing.
